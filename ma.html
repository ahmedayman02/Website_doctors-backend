Introduction to first aid

First aid is the immediate care provided to an injured or ill person before professional medical help arrives. The primary goals of first aid are to preserve life, reduce the severity of the injury, and promote the recovery process. This includes addressing various emergency situations such as wounds, fractures, burns, fainting, and shock. Having a basic understanding of first aid is crucial for everyone, as these skills can be critical when a pe rson requires immediate emergency care.

How to act in an emergency

In emergencies, it's crucial to stay calm and make quick decisions based on the circumstances. Stay in touch with local authorities for guidance. Ensure you have basic supplies and clean water. If you're in a safe place, you may need to stay there until the emergency passes.
